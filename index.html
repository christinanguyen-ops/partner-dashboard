Attribution model — how partner pipeline is credited
How Amplitude credits partner-sourced pipeline
Source path 4 — partner sourced
An opportunity is partner-sourced (source path 4) when the Primary Campaign Source is set to a partner campaign AND the Primary Partner field is populated on the opportunity.
This is what drives the $300K YTD S2 sourced ARR number in the dashboard. Only opps with both fields correctly set are counted.
Why the $8.4M and $300K gap exists
$8.4M is all opps associated with partner campaigns. $300K is only opps that reached S2 with correct attribution tags. The gap is a mix of pre-S2 pipeline and attribution tagging gaps.
W-shaped attribution — marketing influenced
W-shaped attribution credits marketing for first touch, lead creation touch, and opportunity creation touch. Partner marketing campaigns get credit whenever a campaign member is on an opportunity — even if not the primary source.
This means your events influence significantly more pipeline than the sourced number shows. The Partner Scorecard should show both sourced and influenced ARR.
